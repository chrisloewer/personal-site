About Me

My name is Chris Loewer and I love building things. Specifically, I love creating practical, yet elegant, web applications that people will use to enrich their lives.

I became a web developer to exercise my creative expression in a way that has the power to unite people across the globe. From Nairobi to Dallas to Seoul, I love the portability of web technology with its ability to let us impact mass audiences.

As a full stack developer, I can work with everything from the database layer to the front end interface. My goal is to be the middleman between designers and developers.
